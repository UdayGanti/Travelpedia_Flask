Niagra Falls

6-Day 5-Night Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Niagara Falls State Park

Arrive at Niagara Falls and check into your hotel. Spend the day exploring Niagara Falls State Park, which offers stunning views of the falls from various vantage points. You can also take a Maid of the Mist boat tour, which takes you right up to the base of the falls.

Day 2: Niagara-on-the-Lake and Wine Tasting

Drive to Niagara-on-the-Lake, a charming town known for its wineries and historic architecture. Spend the morning exploring the town, visiting local shops, and grabbing lunch. In the afternoon, visit some of the local wineries for wine tastings and vineyard tours.

Day 3: Cave of the Winds and Goat Island

Visit Cave of the Winds, a series of wooden walkways that take you close to the base of Bridal Veil Falls. After Cave of the Winds, head to Goat Island for more stunning views of the falls. You can also take a helicopter tour for an aerial view of the falls.

Day 4: Niagara Falls History Museum and Whirlpool State Park

Visit the Niagara Falls History Museum to learn about the history of the falls and the surrounding area. In the afternoon, head to Whirlpool State Park, which offers great hiking trails and views of the Niagara River Gorge.

Day 5: Niagara Falls Illumination and Skylon Tower

Watch the falls light up at night during the Niagara Falls Illumination. Head to the Skylon Tower for dinner and enjoy panoramic views of the falls and the surrounding area.

Day 6: Niagara Falls Aquarium and Departure

Visit the Niagara Falls Aquarium to see a variety of marine life, including sea lions, sharks, and penguins. Depart Niagara Falls for your next destination.
