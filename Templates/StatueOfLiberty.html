Statue Of Liberty

6-Day 5-Night Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Statue of Liberty

Arrive in New York City and check into your hotel. Head to Battery Park and take a ferry to Liberty Island to visit the Statue of Liberty. Climb to the top of the pedestal or book in advance to visit the crown.

Day 2: Ellis Island and 9/11 Memorial & Museum

Take the ferry to Ellis Island and visit the Ellis Island National Museum of Immigration to learn about the history of American immigration. After Ellis Island, visit the 9/11 Memorial & Museum to pay tribute to those who lost their lives in the 9/11 attacks.

Day 3: Wall Street and One World Trade Center

Explore Wall Street, the financial district of New York City. Visit One World Trade Center and take the elevator to the top to see panoramic views of the city.

Day 4: Central Park and the Metropolitan Museum of Art

Visit Central Park and explore the many attractions it has to offer, such as the Central Park Zoo and the Bethesda Fountain. After Central Park, head to the Metropolitan Museum of Art, one of the world's largest and most comprehensive art museums.

Day 5: Empire State Building and Broadway Show

Visit Central Park and explore the many attractions it has to offer, such as the Central Park Zoo and the Bethesda Fountain. After Central Park, head to the Metropolitan Museum of Art, one of the world's largest and most comprehensive art museums.

Day 6: Brooklyn Bridge and Departure

Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge to see amazing views of Manhattan and Brooklyn. Depart New York City for your next destination.
