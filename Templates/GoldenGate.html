Golden Gate Bridge


6-Day 5-Night Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Arrive in San Francisco and check in to your accommodation. Spend the afternoon exploring the area around the Golden Gate Bridge, including the Presidio and Crissy Field. These areas offer great views of the bridge and the bay. In the evening, head to Fisherman's Wharf to enjoy some seafood and the lively atmosphere.

Day 2: Biking the Bridge

Rent a bike and ride across the Golden Gate Bridge to the charming town of Sausalito on the other side. Explore Sausalito's art galleries, cafes, and shops, and enjoy the waterfront views. Take the ferry back to San Francisco in the late afternoon and have dinner in the city.

Day 3: Alcatraz Island

Take a ferry to Alcatraz Island, the former prison that housed some of America's most notorious criminals. Take the audio tour to learn about the prison's history and its most famous inmates. Return to the city in the early afternoon and spend some time relaxing in Golden Gate Park.

Day 4: Wine Country Tour

Take a day trip to the nearby wine country, such as Napa or Sonoma. Enjoy wine tastings and vineyard tours, and take in the beautiful scenery. Return to San Francisco in the evening and have dinner at a restaurant in one of the city's vibrant neighborhoods, such as the Mission or North Beach.

Day 5: Exploring the City

Spend the day exploring some of San Francisco's other attractions, such as the Golden Gate Park, the Exploratorium, or the de Young Museum. In the evening, head to the Haight-Ashbury neighborhood to experience the city's famous counterculture vibe.

Day 6: Muir Woods and Beach

Take a day trip to Muir Woods, a stunning redwood forest just north of San Francisco. After exploring the woods, head to Muir Beach to enjoy the ocean views and have a picnic. Return to San Francisco in the late afternoon and have a farewell dinner at a nice restaurant.
